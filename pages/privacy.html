PRIVACY POLICY:

This Privacy Policy outlines our practices related to the collection, use, and protection of your information while using ShapeShiftOS.

INFORMATION WE DO NOT COLLECT

ShapeShiftOS does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII), usage data, or any other personal data from users. We believe in preserving your privacy, and our ROM is designed with data minimization principles in mind.

LOCAL DATA HANDLING

The ShapeShiftOS team do not access, collect, or store any data on your device, including but not limited to contacts, messages, call logs, or personal files. We do not track or collect information about your device usage, app usage, or any other activities while using ShapeShiftOS.

THIRD-PARTY APPLICATIONS

ShapeShiftOS may include pre-installed open-source and proprietary apps and services. The privacy policies and data handling practices of these apps and services are governed by their respective developers. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of third-party apps separately.
